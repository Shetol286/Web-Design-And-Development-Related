A Winter Morning

Of the six seasons, the winter is undoubtedly the coldest season in Bangladesh.The winter starts at the end of November and lasts till February. There remains dense fog all around in winter.Sometimes, thefog is so dense that the sun rays cannot get through it.Everything looks hazy. As a resultcommunication system is disrupted.
